Who We Are

We are Neurtion, We value ideas and work hard to turn them into products.

People are at the center of all we do. We operate in self-contained teams comprised of motivated and highly talented individuals from various backgrounds and disciplines. Regardless of the project we're working on, we value supporting one another and sharing our experiences.

Project Management

The project needs a combination of management, meticulousness, and know-how. The project management service provided by Neurtion guarantees that projects are thoroughly handled in accordance with the Neurtion Project Management Method.

Project Implementation

Its aim is to carry out good project analysis and planning, to build strong communication among stakeholders, and to secure the active engagement of the authorities, which we refer to as key users, in the project. By adding value to the projects, the successful completion of the project is ensured.

Custom Software Development

Our Special Software Development service is provided at the customer's request to meet the special software needs he has in relation to his activities; it includes the development and implementation processes running concurrently with the preliminary analysis, detailed analysis, and analysis report studies.

Post-project Support Service

Following Project Assist Service, Project Implementation, and Custom Software Development services; it is a service supplied to organizations to support users and handle new needs. On-site Support, Call Center, Remote Connection, and System Maintenance services are given based on the scale of the organizations' needs.
